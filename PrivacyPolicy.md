# Privacy Policy for D2 Clone Tracker

**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

This privacy policy explains how D2 Clone Tracker ("the App") handles your data. By using the App, you agree to the terms described below.

## 1. Information Collection
The App **does not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services which may collect information used to identify you or your device:

*   **Google AdMob:** Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (like Android Advertising ID) and usage data to serve personalized ads.
*   **Google ML Kit (Text Recognition):** Used on-device to perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition) on Terror Zone images. Some diagnostic and usage data may be shared with Google to improve their services.
*   **Firebase / Google Play Services:** Used for app distribution, updates, and basic analytics.

## 3. Data Usage (APIs)
The App fetches data from the following public sources to provide its core functionality:
*   **diablo2.io API:** To retrieve Diablo Clone progress.
*   **d2tz.info:** To retrieve Terror Zone status images.

The App does not send any of your personal data to these services.

## 4. Permissions
The App requests the following permissions for specific technical reasons:
*   **Internet:** To fetch real-time game data.
*   **Post Notifications:** To alert you when DClone is walking or a Terror Zone starts.
*   **Foreground Service:** To ensure updates are processed reliably in the background.
*   **Schedule Exact Alarm:** To trigger notifications exactly at the start of game events.
*   **Request Ignore Battery Optimizations:** To prevent the system from killing the background tracker.

## 5. Security
The App is designed with security in mind. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of your identity being compromised through our App.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
